What is your responsibility?

Read the following page carefully!
Ultimately, the following will be what you're responsible for, in terms of Templates.

You are responsible for:

  • Writing the script that will do
    • A. pull the required metdata from OpenAsset, and
    • B. populate the export file with the relevant images and metadata.
  • Ensuring that the Customer Success team gives you enough information for you to complete the templates.
  • Communicating with clients with updates on the progress in creating the templates.

What is NOT your responsibility?

  • Making ALL requested changes to the templates, even if the clients really want you to do them. Confirm with your supervisor/CSCs first!!.
