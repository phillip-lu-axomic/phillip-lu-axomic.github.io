OpenAsset

We provide DAM (Digital Asset Management) for our clients. This means that our clients use our products to store all their images, and their associated metadata.

Clients.

OpenAsset's clientele consists of mainly of AEC (architecture, engineering, and construction) industry people. So the images they store are usually of construction or engineering projects.
