Client Expectations
What the heck do they want??

Clients will probably have a full OpenAsset system set up, with files, projects, and their associated metadata populated.
They'll most likely do the following:

  • Project-Based templates - Clients will select multiple projects. The resulting document should contain each project's images and metadata.
  • Image-Based templates - Clients will select multiple images. The resulting document should contain those images, and possibly their associated projects' metadata.
  • Employee-Based templates - Clients will select multiple employees. The resulting document should contain a headshot of the employee, their metadata, as well as the projects they're assigned to, and those projects' associated metadata.
